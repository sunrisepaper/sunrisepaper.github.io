曜升有限公司

曜升有限公司(SUNRISEPAPER LTD)是感熱紙捲生產領域的領導者，已有超過數十年的歷史。 我們為銷售點，收銀機，自助服務終端，ATM和支付處理行業生產其所需的感熱紙。 我們的產品適用於所有市場：零售，金融，酒店，醫療，政府，遊戲等。 產品經由先進的分切能力和印刷機製作，有最佳優良品質保證。

​我們對品質的堅持是我們所有曜升成員的共同信仰，我們的產品可以滿足任何與我們合作的夥伴或客戶，並且能夠隨時提供完美的解決方案，不斷創造新產品及加強品質，持續進步以滿足符合日新月異的各式紙張機器條件。

感熱紙收據可用於各種環境，每天數百萬個地方。 我們公司的獨特優勢在於我們的紙捲價格比其他供應商低， 此外，我們始終提供簡單，準確，可靠的諮詢服務。 我們感謝所有支持我們的客戶！
​
  • 以客戶為導向，注重質量並滿足客戶的需求
  • 積極的態度，基於創新和不斷學習
  • 我們自己和客戶之間的團隊合作，合作和承諾
  • 曜升積極尋求並且促進這些價值觀，實現我們的使命
​

​SUNRISEPAPER LTD have been a leader in thermal paper roll sales for many years. We sale thermal paper for Point of Sale, cash register, kiosk, ATM and payment processing industry. for every market: retail, financial, hospitality, medical, government, gaming, and so on. Now we have advanced slitting capabilities and printing presses, All of them are great resources of our company.

Our mission conveys the desire, shared by all of us who are part of SUNRISE LTD, to identify – along with our partners and customers – solutions to meet their current needs, as well as to create new innovative product features that provide sustainable solutions to a constantly changing.

The most important part of the business is that thermal paper receipts are used in every kind of setting, in millions of places every day. Our company’s unique advantage is that our paper roll price is lower than other suppliers! In addition, we always provide easy, accurate, reliable service. We appreciate and take care of our customers!
​
  • Customer orientation, with an emphasis on quality and meeting our customer’s needs
  • A positive attitude, based on innovation and continuous learning
  • Teamwork, cooperation and commitment, both ourselves and with our customers
  • SUNRISEPAPER LTD actively seeks to promote these values, which will contribute to achieving our mission and making it a reality.
